InkRouter demonstrated its print procurement automation at FSI’s International Vendor Show, held July 22 in New Orleans. Franchise Services Inc. (FSI) put on its annual event for decision makers from printing, signage and marketing technologies to preview print products and services.

Held as part of the Sir Speedy, PIP Printing, MultiCopy and Signal Graphics convention at the Hyatt Regency New Orleans, print franchise owners learned how InkRouter can broaden their network of print providers for print resellers.

“Sir Speedy, PIP and other attendees understand the value InkRouter can bring,” said Robert Godwin, business development director, InkRouter. “They know the markets they serve, and now can serve them with an unlimited range of marketing services.”

Currently offered at no charge to the industry, InkRouter works as a print procurement solution for web-to-print storefronts. Its algorithmic adaptive intelligence matches print orders with print providers based on multiple variables, such as desired location, quality and price points.

“Expanding their production capability and product portfolio without having to buy capital equipment is very attractive to these small business owners,” Godwin commented.

With the ability to easily integrate with popular MIS systems and website platforms, InkRouter’s bi-directional API technology is based on JDF/CIP4 compatible XML definitions.

The growing list of InkRouter clients includes trade printers like Rent a Press, a recently launched wholesale trade printing service.

InkRouter is looking forward to exhibiting at Graph Expo 2016 at booth #2225, to be held Sept. 25-28 at the Orange County Convention Center in Orlando, Fla.
