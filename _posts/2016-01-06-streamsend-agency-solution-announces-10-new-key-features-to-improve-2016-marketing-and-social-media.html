StreamSend, the email marketing service provider, has announced StreamSend Agency Solution, a powerful marketing and social media platform that streamlines campaign implementation, simplifies client account management and boosts bottom lines with an additional revenue stream. Ten key features highlight the platform.

"Marketing agencies face a special set of challenges to do marketing right for their clients,” said Dan Forootan, president of StreamSend Email Marketing. “They need to keep a balance of control, effectiveness and brand promotion while maximizing their clients’ investment. StreamSend Agency Solution is designed for agency needs, with a host of features that make it fast and easy for them to execute sophisticated, effective marketing campaigns now and in 2016.”

1. Agency Dashboard
Agencies can effortlessly manage client campaigns, reporting and invoicing through one simple dashboard. They can offer clients full-service or self-service accounts, plus configure account users with varied permission levels.

2. Customized Agency Account Branding
Agencies can easily customize accounts with their logo and a customized URL. When clients log into their accounts, they will see the agency logo and URL only.

3. Boost the Bottom Line
Agencies can set their own pricing structure and bill clients directly. They can benefit from volume-based discounts and easily assign/update email quotas for each client account. No yearly contracts required.

4. Intuitive and Easy-to-Use
Unlike many unnecessarily complicated email marketing platforms, StreamSend’s intuitive and feature-rich web-based interface makes it easy to roll out integrated campaigns, quickly and effectively.

5. Professionally Designed Templates Optimized for All Screens
StreamSend offers hundreds of professionally designed templates optimized to render beautifully across all screens, from the computer desktop to mobile devices.

6. Design and Code Respect
When emails are uploaded, code integrity will remain 100% intact, without any modification or insertion. Agency design and code is exactly what customers will receive.

7. Advanced Client Templating System
Agencies can convert emails into a “locked” template with predefined editable areas and simple drag and drop functionality that lets them easily add images, text, surveys, video and more.

8. Smart Message Delivery
Smart message delivery options streamline the marketing process by letting agencies create a campaign once and deliver it to email, social and the web with the click of a button.

9. Advanced Features at a Fraction of the Price
Advanced features include triggered emails, advanced segmentation and retargeting, dynamic content, geo-targeting, multi-level reporting, video-in-email, survey embeds, RSS-driven email automation, social sharing and pre-scheduling, ability to track email, social and video from one central location and more.

10. API Access and a Private IP Address at No Additional Cost
StreamSend provides each Agency Account with a private IP address and access to our powerful API at no additional cost.
