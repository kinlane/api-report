The new, fully-responsive design of BlueModus, built in Kentico 9.0, allows the company an amazing platform from which to share its story, range of development and technology services, as well as its world-class website support.

With a mobile-first design, clients, prospects, and agency partners can quickly discover more about BlueModus' audience and market segmentation, its platform priorities, vertical focus areas, and valued methodologies that tie everything together.

"It's a project we're excited about and allows us to strengthen our digital dialogue with our clients and prospects," says President, Marc Maassen, "In addition to discovering more about the case studies (https://www.bluemodus.com/work/case-studies) that span our experience across leading marketing platforms, our clients and prospects will definitely want to spend some time reading our blog (https://www.bluemodus.com/blog). It's full of industry insights and valuable technical tips from our team of experts."

With fresh projects and case studies being produced on a regular basis, and its updated weekly blog, the new BlueModus website will continue to be an online destination for existing and future customers alike. Visit it today at https://www.bluemodus.com.

About BlueModus
Founded in 2001, BlueModus is a digital technology agency that has developed and integrated hundreds of dynamic, interactive applications for our global clients and agency partners, including: Bacardi Global Brands, Cisco Systems, Gap, Aon Hewitt, National Jewish Health, Shire, Bayer Healthcare, Astellas and many others. Typically partnering with digital marketing teams and creative design agencies, BlueModus creates and supports robust Web applications and digital marketing initiatives.
