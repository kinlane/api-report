Medical Design Technologies (MDTECH) announced today it has joined the award-winning Allscripts Developer Program (ADP) to build integration using the Allscripts Open API. The planned integration will enable Allscripts physicians, mid-level providers and nurses to electronically capture charges at the point-of-care and seamlessly synchronize those charges directly into their Allscripts system.

Anytime a charging provider is outside of their Allscripts eco-system, they can use MD Coder as the gateway between Allscripts and the other system holding critical patient information. When a provider has to see patients in various inpatient facilities, MD Coder can seamlessly capture that patient encounter and seamlessly sends it in real time to their Allscripts system to be billed entirely, eliminating any possible charge lag or lost charges. In addition, MD Coder can be integrated into various inpatient systems, nursing facilities and dialysis centers, pulling pertinent patient demographic information and passing this through to the Allscripts system.

ADP partners benefit from a robust set of developer tools and documentation available on the Allscripts Developer Portal. Allscripts also provides technical support and workshops for all developers integrating with its Open API and has shared data with third parties more than a billion times.

Planned App Highlights:

  • Quick - capture charges at the point of care
  • Efficient – deploy in days not months, increase bottom line revenue, minimal training
  • Intuitive – customized application, with a fresh design and intuitive swipes
  • Real-time – updated on the server immediately. New charge, new follow-up, discharge or simply a patient note. All of it, seamless, fast and reliable.
  • Support –reliable US-based team with years of experience, we strive to keep our customers happy
  • Broad – supports 25 specialties from the small solo doc to the large health system

“Joining the Allscripts Developer Program will enable us to help clients by providing them a consistent and reliable mobile charge capture application that is always connected to their Allscripts solution,” said Damon Spiegel, CEO of MDTECH. “Our goal is to provide every Allscripts customer a leading solution that is always connected, always on and always pushing data from MD Coder to Allscripts and vice-versa.”

More information about ADP partners and certified solutions available to Allscripts clients is on the Allscripts Application Store. Developers are encouraged to sign up for a free account on the Allscripts Developer Portal for access to its Open API and to start building innovations for Allscripts users.
