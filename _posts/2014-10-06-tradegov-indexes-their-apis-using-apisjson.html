The International Trade Administration (ITA) has published an APIs.json file, providing a single, machine readable inventory of all APIs available at the federal agency. The agency has also published their APIs.json file to the APIs.io search engine for discovery by developers looking for APIs to integrate into their web and mobile applications.

APIs.json is a machine readable file, using the JSON format, which provides a way to index all APIs available at a company, organization, or government agency. APIs.json is much like the sitemap.xml format which provides an index of websites, but APIs.json is focused exclusively on web APis.

You can see the APIs.json published at developer.trade.gov, and find all supporting documentation, terms of service, and other resources at the agencies developer hub.
