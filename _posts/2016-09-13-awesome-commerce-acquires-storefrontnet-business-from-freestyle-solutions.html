Freestyle Solutions, a leading provider of order and inventory management technology and services for omnichannel and ecommerce retailers, today announced that it has sold the assets of its storefront.net business to Awesome Commerce, a spinoff of Applied Innovations.

Storefront.net is an ecommerce platform used by small and mid-size merchants. Freestyle Solutions has sold thousands of licenses of the Storefront.net software to merchants and currently provides hosting services to users of the storefront.net ecommerce software. Effective immediately with this transaction, Awesome Commerce has acquired the source code and related intellectual property associated with the storefront.net product and Applied Innovations will provide related hosting services following a brief transition period.
