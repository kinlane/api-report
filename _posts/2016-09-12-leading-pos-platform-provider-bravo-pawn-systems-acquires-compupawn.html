Pawn Software Industry Innovators Bravo Pawn Systems is excited to announce the acquisition of Florida-based Vertical Computer Systems, makers of the Compupawn Pawnshop Software. As the top two pawn software platforms in the market, the teams join forces to provide the best services and expertise-backed support in the industry.

Compupawn brings 30+ years of experience delivering pawn software solutions. Under the leadership of Brett Howell, President and CEO of Vertical Computer Systems, the Compupawn brand developed a strong foothold in the pawn industry over the years and built many relationships with pawnbrokers worldwide. With an emphasis in delivering industry distinguished solutions with top-of-the-line customer service, Compupawn aligns perfectly with Bravo’s core mission to revolutionize the pawn industry.

Bravo Pawn Systems’ Founder and CEO Steve Mack says, “For years, Brett and the Compupawn team has been one of the leading pawn software vendors by providing top notch services and building strong relationships across the pawn industry through their dedication to pawnbrokers worldwide. I’m excited to now bring the best minds from both Compupawn and Bravo under one roof in order to further enhance Bravo’s offering – propelling our mission to deliver the best pawn software on the market with out-of-this-world support.”

Together, the two companies will partner up to continue the Bravo Pawn System’s goal of providing pawnbrokers worldwide with the best Point-of-Sale platform to elevate their businesses. The teams plan on combining the best features of both platforms under the Bravo brand in order to provide the most robust and powerful pawn software ever provided for the pawn industry.

In the past five years, the Bravo platform has been the fastest growing pawn software ever because of exclusive features like:

  •     Accurate, fast, and data-rich conversions
  •     Real-time task manager to automate store processes
  •     Certified and fully integrated eBay API
  •     Exclusive access to buya.com, the only pawnshop online marketplace
  •     Customizable reporting and complete financials
  •     First Mobile App. in the industry for consumers and employees
  •     Advanced customer, product, and financial analytics
  •     Built with Microsoft’s newest and most reliable cloud technology
  •     Complete training options for new employees, managers, and owners
