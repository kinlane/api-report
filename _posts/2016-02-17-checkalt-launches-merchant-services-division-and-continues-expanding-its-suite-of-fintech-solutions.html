CheckAlt today announced the launch of its Merchant Services Division. CheckAlt Merchant Services will offer a variety of credit card and other electronic payment processing solutions for small and medium sized businesses, as well as, a white label program for financial institutions looking to meet the payment processing needs of their clients with a program which helps maximize relationship value and grow their bottom line.

“We see tremendous growth in the merchant services area and an opportunity to leverage CheckAlt’s innovation lab and infrastructure to provide cutting edge transaction processing solutions and equipment to brick and mortar and online businesses throughout the US,” said Shai Stern, CEO & co-chairman at CheckAlt. “In addition, we’re now able to deliver a comprehensive, white labeled merchant services program to financial institutions.”

CheckAlt Merchant Services Solutions Include

  • Credit & Debit Card Payment Processing
  • Payment Gateways
  • Gift Cards & Loyalty Programs
  • Online Transaction Processing
  • Point of Sale Systems
  • Automated Clearing House Check Drafting and Payment Services
  • Check Guarantee & Conversion Services
  • Merchant Cash Advance
  • eCheck Processing

All trends indicate consumer spending will continue to increase (post-recession) and their use of cash will decline as credit card usage increases. This means accepting and processing credit card transactions will continue to grow in importance for every business owner. CheckAlt Merchant Services is positioned to quickly deliver competitively priced processing solutions, exceptional client support and the latest equipment technology. Their team of experienced payment professionals will customize a solution to fit the needs (and budget) of each business. They are committed to helping businesses get up and running and begin processing transactions as quickly as possible.

To get started, or for more information, please visit http://www.checkaltmerchantservices.com.

CheckAlt Merchant Services uses this institutional knowledge and expertise to offer card processing solutions, equipment and other payment processing technology that is cutting edge, competitively priced and designed to satisfy the needs of business owners.
