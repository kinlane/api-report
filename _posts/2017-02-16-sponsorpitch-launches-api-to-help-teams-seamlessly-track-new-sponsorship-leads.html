SponsorPitch, the leading online platform helping sponsorship sales professionals research and connect with new sponsors, is pleased to announce the launch of the first application programming interface (API) specifically designed for sponsorship and marketing professionals. Well known as being the daily “go to source” for all things sponsorship, SponsorPitch’s new API will enable fast, flexible and on-demand access to the industry’s largest real time deal database.

This launch of SponsorPitch’s enterprise features follows a successful beta of the platform’s new API and export features with key clients throughout the fall of last year including the research and IT departments at many of the world’s top sponsorship, marketing and advertising agencies.

“Since SponsorPitch’s inception, we’ve been focused on bringing a new standard of innovation to the $50 billion sponsorship space,” said Kris Mathis, Founder and CEO of SponsorPitch. "Access to the world’s largest sponsorship data stream empowers our partners to synthesize large volumes of data, run deeper analytics and bring richer, more accurate and compelling sponsorship narratives to their new business pitches in record time.”

SponsorPitch’s new API and on-demand export features are available to the public starting today with full API documentation also available upon request: info(at)sponsorpitch(dot)com. Using SponsorPitch’s API, IT and research teams can now produce regular, automated sponsorship reports with unlimited API calls that pull key sponsorship deal data into internal databases for customized research and analysis.

ABOUT SPONSORPITCH
SponsorPitch, founded in 2009, is a real-time online recommendation engine for sponsorship that uses predictive intelligence and analytics to help over 60,000 members find the right partners faster. Powered by the world's largest searchable, real-time sponsorship deal database, the online platform helps professionals find, manage and measure marketing partnerships at a fraction of the time and cost of traditional methods through an innovative, industry-leading feature set that includes search and filtering, in-depth company sponsorship profiles, reports, recommendations, exports, an API and much more. SponsorPitch is headquartered in New York City. For additional information, please visit SponsorPitch.com.
