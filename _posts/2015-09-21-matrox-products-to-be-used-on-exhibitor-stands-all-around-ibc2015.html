Matrox today announced that exhibitors throughout IBC2015 (Amsterdam, Sept. 11–15) will be using products from all three of its divisions on their stands. Matrox Imaging’s Orion HD and VIO frame grabbers will be featured by ClearView Imaging. Matrox Graphics Inc.’s Maevex™ H.264 decoder will be used by Panasonic with their AW-HE130 PTZ cameras and Matrox C680 multi-display graphics cards will be used by Quantel to power 4K screens. Matrox Video’s Xmio.3 multi-channel SDI I/O cards, M264 multi-channel 4:2:2 10-bit H.264 encoder card, MicroQuad™ multiviewers and MC-100™ mini converters will be used by Vizrt. Matrox Monarch™ HD H.264 encoders will be used at the Wowza Media Systems™ stand in a streaming demonstration.

ClearView Imaging — stand 8.E17
Matrox Orion HD and VIO frame grabbers will be featured by UK distributor ClearView Imaging, experts in machine vision, who help OEMs and end users develop vision systems from scratch or source components. Orion HD is a high performance frame grabber board for vision applications that supports HD/SD video inputs and outputs including 1080p/60. Matrox Vio is a family of PCIe boards that handle HD/SD analog and SDI video in imaging systems.

Panasonic — stand 9.C45
Matrox Maevex H.264 decoders will be used to decode and display live feeds from Panasonic AW-HE130 PTZ cameras, showcasing the AW-HE130’s exceptional-quality capture and transmission in its best light. Panasonic AW-HE130 pan/tilt/zoom cameras are ideal for demanding video-over-IP applications in sports, rental/staging, news and enterprise to transmit multi-stream H.264 HD video and audio at up to 1080/50p at 25Mbps. Matrox Maevex H.264 decoders provide cost-effective AV-over-IP decoding. Solid state, fanless design and low power requirements make Maevex a stable, reliable decode solution and the Matrox PowerStream™ control software and API let administrators control Maevex decoders from a centralized location, as well as easily change Panasonic camera streams output from given decoders.

Quantel — stand 7.G20
Quantel chose the Matrox C680 multi-display graphics card to play out promotional videos on 84-inch 4K screens in their stand because it is a cost-effective, simple-to-use card with multiple 4K outputs. Matrox C680, a single-slot, PCIe x16 graphics card that drives up to six Ultra HD/4K displays or projectors can also enable 12-monitor configurations when combined with a second C680 board. The board-to-board framelock feature ensures synchronization of all displays to reduce tearing on digital signage and video walls.

Vizrt — stand 7.A20
Matrox Xmio.3 multi-channel SDI I/O cards will be hard at work under-the-hood on the Vizrt stand helping to showcase the breathtaking content created by the full 4K rendering power of Viz Engine. Vizrt will also present a technology demonstration of Viz Engine that incorporates the Matrox M264 multi-channel 4:2:2 10-bit H.264 encoder card to provide contribution-quality IP playout. In addition, Matrox MicroQuad multiviewers and MC-100 quad-split SDI to HDMI mini converters will be used by Vizrt to display SDI feeds on HDMI monitors. Matrox MicroQuad is a four-channel SDI-to-HDMI multiviewer for 3G/HD/SD that lets broadcasters and A/V professionals use an affordable HDMI display to view up to four SDI video signals and show or hide labels and VU meters. Its powerful 10-bit scaling engine and advanced filtering algorithms ensure a crisp, artifact-free monitoring experience. Matrox MC-100 is a dual SDI-to-HDMI mini converter that supports a wide range of display resolutions through 3G, Dual Link, HD, and SD-SDI.

Wowza Media Systems™ — stand 3.B18
A Matrox Monarch HD streaming and recording appliance will be used at the Wowza Media Systems™ stand in a streaming demonstration. Monarch HD is part of the Works With Wowza™ partner program from Wowza. Joint Matrox Monarch HD–Wowza Streaming Engine™ software customers benefit from enhanced integration and ease-of-use, simplifying their live streaming workflows. Monarch HD users can simply download an XML file containing all relevant credentials to connect to the Wowza sever via the Wowza Streaming Engine Manager admin panel, select an encoding preset and begin streaming. Matrox Monarch HD is a small, easy-to-use H.264 encoder designed for professional video producers who need to simultaneously stream a live event and record a mastering-quality version for post-event editing.
