CINC (Commissions Inc) announced the redesign of their popular Seller Suite today. The solution is powered by Onboard Informatics, the leader in data-driven engagement. The Suite, provides home owners with an Accurate Home Valuation, a comparable list of recently sold properties, and a Neighborhood Pulse with Trend recapped in monthly updates. Early results from this redesign are impressive: 

  •     18.4% conversion rate (visitor to lead) for seller leads at CINC
  •     270% increased lead generation over 60-day trial
  •     18% Facebook Ad Conversion Rate

“We really wanted to create a complete experience for consumers on behalf of our clients,” said Mike Gullo, CINC Director of Product. “Our Marketing Services Team places ads designed to drive leads to a specific landing page and capture their contact information for ongoing nurture campaigns around their neighborhood of interest. By offering a complete solution that’s modern and personalized, we’ve seen staggering results.”

CINC’s Seller Suite gives potential home sellers a better understanding of the current value of their home using Onboard’s property API. A personalized report is generated for each user in just 30 seconds. Upon sharing the home’s address and answering a few additional questions, home owners are also able to receive an accurate home valuation, a list of recently sold properties, and monthly updates so they stay in the know.

"Since advertising with Seller Suites, just over 3 months ago days ago, my Team has seen a significant improvement in both our volume of monthly seller leads and our contact and close ratio. Prior to using Social Sell, we averaged between 12-16 seller leads monthly. Within the first month of using Social Sell we doubled that number to 28 and in the last month we have brought in 39 seller leads. We noticed a bump ranging from 5-9 new seller leads monthly from the remarketing campaign with CINC as well,” said CINC client, Brent Chapuis, Team Owner of Coastal Realty Group located in Biloxi, MS.
