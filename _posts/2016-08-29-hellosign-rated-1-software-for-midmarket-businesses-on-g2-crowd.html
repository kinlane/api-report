HelloSign, the easiest way to electronically sign legally binding documents, has been ranked the best software solution for mid-market businesses by G2 Crowd, a popular business software review platform, in their annual “Top 25 Mid-Market Software Products” list released today. The list showcases the software that mid-market business users rated highly, based on customer satisfaction score. This ranking represents HelloSign’s ongoing commitment to creating a product that provides tremendous value to businesses of all sizes.

To learn more about HelloSign in the latest G2 Crowd list, click here: http://goo.gl/U5mYBb

"Mid-market users awarded HelloSign with such high "likelihood to recommend" scores that it landed the #1 spot on the ‘Top 25 Mid-Market Software Products’ list,” said Marty Duffy, Director of Research at G2 Crowd.

This top rating comes only one week after being named the #1 eSignature solution for small businesses in G2 Crowd's “Top 50 Small-Businesses Products,” making HelloSign the #1 eSignature solution for both small and mid-market businesses. According to G2 Crowd, customer satisfaction scores are determined based on reviewers likeliness to recommend the product to a friend or colleague. User reviews for HelloSign from mid-market businesses on G2 Crowd praise the ease of use, increased speed to ROI, and enterprise level functionality.

“We continue to dedicate ourselves to providing the perfect solutions for businesses of any size who are ready to leave behind costly paperwork systems and clunky document flows,” says Joseph Walla, CEO and Co-founder of HelloSign. “As the best overall software for mid-sized businesses, it’s clear that HelloSign is providing value that extends far beyond just eSignatures. This includes functionality like powerful integrations, robust team management capabilities, bank-level security, intelligent document flows, and our developer-friendly API.”

G2 Crowd is the world’s leading business software review platform, leveraging its 92,000+ user reviews read by nearly 600,000 software buyers each month to help them make better purchasing decisions. By bringing the collective power of trusted peers to the forefront, business buyers now have transparency when evaluating B2B software technologies. In addition, G2 Crowd packages insights from expert peers, everyday users, and aggregated data to score competitive products on “The Grid.”
