Apigee, developer of an intelligent API platform for digital business, today announced a new co-marketing partnership with Delphix to help organizations more quickly gain the benefits of using the Apigee Edge API management software and Delphix Data as a Service solution together. Exposing Delphix APIs in Apigee can simplify and accelerate the end-to-end development, testing, provisioning and cloud on-boarding of data-driven applications.

Delphix develops Data as a Service (DaaS) software that helps customers release applications up to 10 times faster by delivering secure, virtualized data across the application lifecycle. Apigee Edge is a leading API management platform that empowers organizations to securely deliver and manage APIs, with agility and at scale. Using Apigee and Delphix software together, customers may achieve the benefits of enterprise-grade API management coupled with the portability of self-service virtual data provisioning.

“Data delivery and data security are top of mind for CIOs as demands on application and infrastructure teams continue to increase,” said Marc Odenweller, vice president of channels, Delphix. “We believe working with Apigee can help mutual customers realize even greater efficiencies for building apps that drive business value.”

“In today’s accelerated digital economy, data is the fuel, APIs provide the point of control – and the faster an organization can use data and APIs to build modern, connected apps, the more competitive they can be,” said Ed Anuff, Apigee executive vice president of strategy. “We look forward to working with Delphix to help organizations realize the benefits of accelerating the end-to-end application development process with Apigee and Delphix.”

For more information on the potential benefits of using Apigee and Delphix software together, see the recent post on the Delphix blog.

About Apigee
Apigee® (NASDAQ: APIC) provides an intelligent API platform for digital business. Many of the world's largest organizations select Apigee to enable their digital business, including more than 25 percent of the Fortune 100, five of the top six Global 2000 retail companies, and five of the top 10 global telecommunications companies. Apigee customers include global enterprises such as Walgreens, Burberry, Morningstar, and First Data. Apigee is headquartered in San Jose, California and has over 400 employees worldwide.
