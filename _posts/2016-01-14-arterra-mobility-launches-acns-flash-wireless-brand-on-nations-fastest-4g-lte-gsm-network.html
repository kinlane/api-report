Arterra Mobility announced today it has partnered with ACN, the world’s largest direct seller of telecommunications, energy, and other essential services, on the recent launch of its Flash Wireless MVNO brand on the nation’s fastest 4G LTE GSM network. Flash Wireless gives its customers the power to choose affordable, flexible rate plans, including family plans, contract or no contract options, a robust device selection or the ability to bring your own device – all from the nation’s best networks. The addition of GSM capabilities gives Flash customers even more choices to meet their wireless needs.

Arterra Mobility powers Flash Wireless’ mobile services including: advanced single-line and family rate plans, specialized and customized business rules with robust APIs, vigorous reporting capabilities, support for a high-end e-commerce platform and website, and aggregated network capabilities on a robust nationwide GSM-based 4G and 4G LTE network.

“By partnering with Arterra Mobility, we chose a strong partner with extensive experience launching MVNOs that we knew would create the kind of solution we needed to take our Flash Wireless business – and customer experience – to the next level,” said Robert Stevanovski, Chairman and Co-Founder of ACN, Inc. “To date, our experience with Arterra has been excellent, and we’re excited about our future together.”

“We are proud to be partnering with Flash Wireless, one of the country’s most successful MVNOs, as they extend their already successful wireless services into GSM,” said Daniel Neal, CEO and Founder of Arterra Mobility.” By combining Arterra’s experience launching highly-scalable and customized wireless solutions, Flash Wireless’ strong brand and distribution model, and the power of a robust nationwide GSM-based 4G and 4G LTE network, Flash Wireless will offer a level of unparalleled wireless service to customers throughout the United States,”

As a highly respected wireless enablement and solutions integration firm, Arterra Mobility has a long history of building competitively differentiated services and systems for its clients. Arterra Mobility’s expertise goes well beyond typical BSS/OSS-focused providers. By molding raw network capabilities and proprietary network design into unique and elegant customer-facing solutions, Arterra Mobility helps its clients create award-winning services that accelerate their customers’ go-to-market plans and position them to compete successfully in the marketplace.

The Arterra Mobility MVNE/MVNO/MVNA solutions enable its clients to deploy a custom wireless solution under their own brand with rapid time-to-market, the lowest possible capital expenditure, and the lowest total cost of ownership. Arterra Mobility provides full wireless service lifecycle support, including product design, implementation, testing, deployment, service management and service optimization. The full Arterra Mobility suite of services includes real-time rating and controls; data filtering; mobile walleting and location services; custom web interfaces; CRM and customer self-care; carrier integration; distribution and payment support; multi-language customer care; and forward and reverse logistics. Even experienced international carriers take advantage of the proprietary patented technology of Arterra Mobility; capabilities and expertise that extend through the entire value chain; and robust existing integrations with both CDMA and GSM major wireless network operators.
