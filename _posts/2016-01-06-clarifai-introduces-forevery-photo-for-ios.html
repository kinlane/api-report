Clarifai, the artificial intelligence company reinventing visual recognition, today announced the availability of Forevery Photo, a free mobile app that takes users on a personalized journey through every memory saved on their camera roll.

Forevery uses the latest artificial intelligence technology to give users a completely personalized and unique photo discovery experience. By understanding what is important to each user, the app automatically applies descriptive labels to photos and organizes them for easy storytelling and sharing.

“Clarifai is already known for providing the most powerful artificial intelligence technology available to the world’s largest companies,” said Matthew Zeiler, Founder and CEO. “By publishing Forevery Photo on iOS, we are putting the power of our technology in the hands of the consumer.”

Forevery is powered by Clarifai’s advanced technology based on neural networks and algorithms designed to simulate how the brain functions. These models learn from each new image and video they see, training the algorithm to become better at recognizing any one of tens of thousands of concepts in real-time. These concepts range from broad themes like emotions and ideas, to specific subjects like people, places, things, and time.

Features Include:

  • Personalization - Customize the app to understand what’s most important to you
  • Organization - Forevery automatically organizes your photos by broad themes like emotions and ideas, as well as people, places, things, and time
  • Searchability - Browse by related photos and get your daily dose of inspiration sorted by time, place, or visual appearance
  • Shareability - Share the memories that matter most with the people who matter most. Send photo stories to anyone at the tap of a button

About Clarifai
Clarifai is a pioneer in artificial intelligence technology, offering powerful APIs to augment intelligence in a new generation of machine learning applications. Founded in 2013 with the world’s leading image recognition technology, Clarifai has quickly expanded to provide a deep understanding of image and video content at scale for the world’s most innovative developers and companies.
