Green Collar Foods (GCF), a Detroit, MI-based indoor vertical farming company, is launching a $1.5 million equity financing campaign on AgFunder, the leading agriculture-focused online investment platform. The company is in the controlled environment agriculture (CEA) sector and deploying an innovative network of franchised inner-city farming facilities. The GCF vision is to empower inner-city farmers with an affordable CEA business solution that is technologically intuitive and financially profitable.

This enables continuous learning for GCF and its clients to extract efficiencies, record plant recipes and improve yields. The GCF AgCloud was built in collaboration with technology partner and investor Shaping Cloud.

The main aim of this technology is to enable anyone to operate an inner-city indoor farm while continuously learning from the process. GCF is passionate about democratizing inner-city CEA along with increasing the sustainability of each new location.
