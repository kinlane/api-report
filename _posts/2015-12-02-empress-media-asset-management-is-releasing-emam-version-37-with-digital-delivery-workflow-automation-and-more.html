Empress Media Asset Management will be showcasing the beta release for version 3.7 of its flagship eMAM™ at the Content and Communication World show booth 1342. This latest version incorporates many updates and features as suggested from customers, marketing partners, and technology partners to enhance eMAM’s brand as an integrated solution which addresses the complete workflow requirements of its customers.

eMAM has developed options and packages for delivery and publishing of media to Web, mobile and set-top box platforms. eMAM adds value to traditional cloud publishing workflows by integrating the production process with selective content publishing to minimize cloud storage costs, while providing rapid access and publishing tools from traditional storage systems and low cost permanent archives.

“Beyond our ability to develop customized portals and experiences, we are excited to automate publishing and sharing of content on popular social media and Web publishing platforms and systems,” commented CEO Wendy Wang.

The new version introduces numerous improvements, including:

  • additional preview controls for subtitling, audio channels, and video quality selection
  • faster and easier uploads with no size restrictions
  • updated mobile interfaces: Android tablet interface in the Play Store and iPad app in App Store
  • safe project archive and deletion, so needed media will not be removed from shared storage
  • job priority settings
  • category and project search
  • a new interface theme

COO David Miller said, “Our global marketing strategy relies on our system integrators and technology partners. The new workflow designer can help our integration partners easily set up automated processes based on rules and conditions, while the RESTful API will aid technology integrations. We will be featuring updated integrations with Spectra Logic, Quantum, HP, and Adobe at the show.”

About Empress Media Asset Management
Empress Media Asset Management, LLC is a privately held company providing media asset management and workflow management solutions for broadcast, media, government, and corporate clients including its eMAM Online, eMAM Vault, eMAM Workgroup, and eMAM Enterprise product lines. It is part of the Empress family of companies, which includes Empress Info Tech, specializing in customized software development, Empress Digital, one of the largest US distributors of blank recording media and supplies, and Real Big Hits, which helps aspiring creatives get discovered, distributed, and funded.
