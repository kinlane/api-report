URL shortener, and social bookmarking API Bitly has made some changes to their privacy policy to keep with the times, and change with their evolving business model. As of August 8, 2014, the following updates will be implemented:

  • A clear explanation of the data we collect
  • Assurance that the data is not linked to any Personally Identifiable Information (PII)
  • How the anonymous data we collect will be shared
  • The privacy policy includes a link to the Consumer Choice opt-out page on our website so that you can manage all of your browser opt out preferences
  • We are committed to keeping Bitly’s free services available to all users. However it’s important to continue to offer the services they have come to love while still being able to thrive as a business.

Bit.ly will also be updating their Terms of Service (TOS) to keep in sync with the privacy policy, as well as their business goals. You can find more information about the changes to the Bit.ly privacy policy on their blog.
