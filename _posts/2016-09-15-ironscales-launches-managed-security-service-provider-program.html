IRONSCALES, the first and only automated email phishing response solution to combine human intelligence with machine learning, today announced the launch of its managed security service provider (MSSP) program. Through the program, MSSPs can now partner with IRONSCALES to provide their enterprise, mid-market and small business customers with gamified phishing simulations, employee awareness training, real-time threat intelligence and automated incident response. With IRONSCALES, MSSPs can detect and stop dangerous and destructive zero-day attacks, such as ransomware, malware, bots, spam, spoofing, and pharming for their clients.

According to MarketIntelReports, the global MSSP market is expected to reach almost $41 billion in revenue by 2022, as demand for outsourced cybersecurity skyrockets in conjunction with the proliferation of threats and attacks targeting businesses of all sizes. In response, IRONSCALES MSSP program will enable MSSPs to plan continuous phishing simulations and training programs for all clients at once; identify suspicious emails and trigger immediate automated responses; share phishing intelligence across all accounts, and provide deep forensic capabilities such as Multi-AV scans and malware-detecting sandbox technology to quarantine suspicious emails.
