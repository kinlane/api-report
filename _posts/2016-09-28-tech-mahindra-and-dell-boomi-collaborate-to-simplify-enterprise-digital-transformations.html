Dell Boomi and Tech Mahindra today announced an alliance that enables organizations to more easily implement digital strategies and drive business transformation. As a “Dell Boomi Certified System Integrator,” Tech Mahindra, provider of innovative and customer-centric information technology services and solutions, is now able to offer its customers a platform that integrates any combination of cloud and on-premises applications without software, appliances or coding.

“Tech Mahindra specializes in enabling digital transformations for customers across industries that include manufacturing, telecom, healthcare and retail,” said Pritam Parvatkar, Global Head of Alliances & Partnerships at Tech Mahindra. “Our Customer Experience group’s large portfolio of integration solutions play a vital role in connecting on-premises and cloud applications to offer a seamless process experience for all stakeholders. Dell Boomi is the leader in the integration Platform as a Service (iPaaS) space, and our partnership with them will enable our enterprise clients to dramatically improve their customers’ experience across sales, service and marketing.”

The Dell Boomi Partner Program supports a global network of partners who recognize such a partnership will better enable them to support their own customers with the Dell Boomi AtomSphere® integration Platform as a Service (iPaaS) solution. Program members include System Integrators, Independent Software Vendors and Managed Service Providers whose customers have a need for automating business processes by integrating applications.

With Dell Boomi iPaaS, Tech Mahindra’s solutions will now allow customers to:

  • Quickly and painlessly connect any combination of cloud and on-premises applications.
  • Create new integrations using a simple drag-and-drop interface.
  • Leverage Boomi Suggest to harness the knowledge of the Dell Boomi community to decrease new integration mapping times.
  • Use pre-built and standardized connectors to speed integration development.
  • Manage all integration processes from a single, unified view.
  • Easily test new integrations before rolling them out.

“Dell Boomi AtomSphere is a critical enabler of the digital transformations that enterprises increasingly recognize are essential for maintaining productivity, scalability and competitiveness,” said Chris McNabb, general manager at Dell Boomi. “By joining our network of partners, Tech Mahindra can now offer our platform to customers across its diverse global customer base.”
