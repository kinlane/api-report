Tradier, Inc., the leading brokerage-as-a-service company, today announced that predictive analytics platform, WooTrader has integrated with the Tradier API to enable users to purchase and sell stocks directly from within its system. WooTrader’s weighted predictive analytics model helps investors make more informed investments by leveraging hundreds of stock market indicators to generate weighted rankings that reflect current stock market trends. The Tradier brokerage platform integration will enable Wootrader users to benefit from a more frictionless trading experience with key stock rankings and ability to seamlessly buy or sell trades from the platform.

Wootrader uses over 350 different technical, fundamental, analyst and options markets indicators to analyze stocks daily. Through its proprietary platform, it measures their performance daily to generate weighted stock rankings that better reflect current stock market trends. Investors leverage Wootrader for investment strategies and with Tradier, they can now execute on timely trades from one, easy-to-use dashboard.

“Wootrader was established to democratize trading for the modern trader and with Tradier, our company is able to extend its commitment to making advanced trading tools accessible to everyone," said Atanas Stoyanov, Founder and CEO of WooTrader, Inc. "Our integration with the Tradier API arms our users with brokerage capabilities so they can make data-informed investment decisions and then immediately act on those decisions.”

“WooTrader, Inc. helps new and veteran investors alike make smarter trading decisions with its ability to analyze hundreds of different stock indicators as the market fluctuates and share comprehensive and weighted stock information in an easy-to-understand and easy-to-apply manner,” said Craig Russell, Chief Evangelist and SVP of Product at Tradier, Inc. “The integration of Wootrader with our brokerage API brings all-in-one, user-friendly trading solutions to retail investors and grants them the ability to easily and immediately go from evaluating a stock ranking, to placing a stock purchase or sale.”

Wootrader uses over 350 different technical, fundamental, analyst and options markets indicators to analyze and rank stocks daily. With its integration with Tradier’s brokerage API, users can immediately execute on any of these trades.
