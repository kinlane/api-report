ResMan® will be exhibiting Affordable Housing, the latest addition to the fastest growing online property management software at the 2016 National Council of State Housing Agencies Conference and Showplace on September 26-27. Attendees are invited to Booth #18 to learn more about the key features and technologies that manage, monitor, and measure affordable housing assets through ResMan Affordable Housing.

A highlight of ResMan's booth will be a demonstration of the Robust Reporting features, allowing owners and operators to analyze industry-specific data with customized reporting to offer instant insights, ultimately protecting and growing investments.

"ResMan Affordable supplies easy-to-use HUD Reporting via TRACS including HAP processing, Special Claims and Repayment Agreements ensuring assistance payments are received and additional revenue is realized," states Jeff Dean, Product Manager. "ResMan Affordable integrates with state housing finance agencies allowing for streamlined Tax Credits reporting requirements."

NCSHA conference attendees are encouraged to meet Product Manager Jeff Dean and representatives from the ResMan Sales Team all day on September 26 and until 9:45AM on September 27 at the Fontainebleau Miami to learn more about the reimagined customer experience that directly impacts property NOI.

About ResMan
ResMan® is the fastest growing property management software solution in the multifamily industry, providing easy-to-use automation tools that improve operational effectiveness and productivity for Conventional and Affordable Housing Property Management. The SaaS provider focuses on Service First in everything, with intuitive software and industry’s leading customer experience. Built on a single platform; ResMan® delivers a highly intuitive solution to meet both operational and accounting needs for the multifamily owner/operator. In addition, the ResMan+ Innovation Network is an open API actively promoting best-of-breed solutions to meet each customer’s unique needs.
