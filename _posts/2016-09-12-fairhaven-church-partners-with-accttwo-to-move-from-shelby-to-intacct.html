Previous Business Challenges Faced by Fairhaven Church:

  • Keeping data current and accurate was virtually impossible using their legacy accounting system (Shelby).
  • With three campuses, managing fixed assets between locations was a challenge.
  • Budgets had to be built in Excel, which was a time consuming process for both staff and leadership.
  • Manual payment approval processes were distracting staff from mission-critical tasks.

Reasons for Selecting Intacct:

  • Fairhaven's leaders will have access to data in real-time, through easy to read dashboard reports, and from anywhere one can gain Internet access.
