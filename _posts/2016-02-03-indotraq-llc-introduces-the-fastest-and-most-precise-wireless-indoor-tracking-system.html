IndoTraq™ LLC, a leading provider of indoor positioning technology, introduces the fastest and most precise wireless indoor tracking system in the world at CES 2016 in Las Vegas, NV. The revolutionary new and powerful indoor-patented technology called HSKT™ (High-Speed Kalman Tracking) combines an Inertial Measurement Unit (IMU) and an Ultra-Wideband (UWB) position tracking system, which in turn delivers an affordable, high speed and extremely precise tracking system to the industry. The technology has a precision of +/- 5mm; an indoor range of 100m and outdoors of 300m; with update rates of 200 times per second.

A press conference will be held on Wednesday, January 6th, at 10:30 AM at Bellini 2006 in the Sands Expo. The press conference will include a live demonstration of the technology.

Michael Hamilton, Founder and CTO at IndoTraq™ LLC states, "Throughout the industry, we've heard phenomenal excitement about the extreme precision of our solution. We will be demonstrating the very high level of precision of the VRTraq™ solution at the show by using a smartphone running a virtual reality game. With this technology the user is finally able to get up and move freely around in the virtual reality experience."

IndoTraq™ will be releasing a development kit and API during Q1 2016. This will include the tools for app developers who can now build long-awaited precise indoor positioning solutions and apps. There are a host of commercial applications in many verticals that can leverage this technology, some of which are virtual and augmented reality, motion capture, wearable tracking, manufacturing, and autonomous robots.

"We are working closely with various technology players to make indoor positioning truly ubiquitous and global, and available for everyone. We are proud to be the provider of the fastest and the most precise indoor tracking solution for all developers," said David Sym-Smith, CEO, IndoTraq™ LLC. "Our solution also has the capability to add value to existing infrastructure investments such as iBeacon or Wi-Fi to help deliver the best indoor positioning performance."

Product Highlights and Benefits at a Glance

  •     Tracking system provides sub 5mm precision
  •     Update rate of up to 200 Hz (5ms latency)
  •     Signal penetrates obstacles and walls
  •     Indoor range 100m / Outdoor range 300m
  •     Auto configuration
  •     IndoTraq™ API and tools are available for third-party app developers.

About IndoTraq™ LLC
Founded in 2014, IndoTraq™ LLC has developed the fastest and most precise indoor tracking system in the world. It has a precision of +/- 5 mm indoor through walls, range of 100 m and updates at a rate of 200 Hz (200 times per second). The outdoor range can reach up to 300 m. A host of commercial applications in many verticals can leverage this technology including virtual and augmented reality, motion capture, wearable tracking, manufacturing, autonomous robots, and motion based analytics.
