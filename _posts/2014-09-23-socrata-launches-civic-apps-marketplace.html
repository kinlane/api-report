Civic data platform Socrata has launched a new marketplace dedicated to showcasing web and mobile apps that are build for civic engagement.

Similar to other marketplaces like iTunes or Chrome, Socrata wants to highlight useful apps that can be used in city, county, state or federal government operations.

dEach app has details on whether it is web, mobile, or desktop, as well as whether it is paid and certified by Socrata. Developers can submit their civic applications, and have listed in a number of marketplace categories.

The Socrata Apps Marketplace is filling a need for civic focused applications, being build on the growing amount of open data, and APIs being released at all levels of government.
