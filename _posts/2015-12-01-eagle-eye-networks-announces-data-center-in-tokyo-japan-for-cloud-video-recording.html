Eagle Eye Networks™, Inc. today announced the opening of its data center in Tokyo, Japan. Businesses using the Eagle Eye Security Camera Video Management System (VMS) can now store their video exclusively in Japan.

In other news today, Eagle Eye Networks announced that ALSOK Adopted Eagle Eye's cloud video surveillance for ALSOK Customers.

Other relevant features include:

  • Japanese-language GUI. The user interface has been fully internationalized into Japanese.
  • Secure mobile access. Eagle Eye Networks offers unrivaled security and encryption, with secure remote access via smartphones and tablets, as well as motion and system alerts.
  • Broad IP and analog camera support. The Eagle Eye Cloud Security Camera VMS offers a wide array of supported IP and analog cameras

“The Japan data center is our fourth data center worldwide. It’s important that video and security data be stored within the country for many regions,” said Dean Drako, Eagle Eye Networks’ president and CEO. “Further, our Japanese user interface development shows our strong commitment to serving our customers in Japan.”

Eagle Eye Data Centers
The cloud-based Eagle Eye Security Camera VMS is powered by multiple Tier IV data centers. The infrastructure is been specifically designed for video and is managed directly by Eagle Eye personnel to provide maximum security, performance, and availability. The data centers support developer activity for the cloud-based Eagle Eye Video API, which allows businesses and developers to integrate camera video into their applications without having to deploy complex infrastructure. Eagle Eye Networks has four data centers, located in: Austin, Texas; Santa Clara, California; Montréal, Canada; and Tokyo, Japan.

About Eagle Eye Networks
Eagle Eye Networks delivers the first on-demand cloud based security and business intelligence video management system (VMS) providing both cloud and on-premise recording. Eagle Eye also provides a cloud video API for integrations and application development. The Eagle Eye Platform offers secure, encrypted recording, camera management, mobile viewing and alerts – all 100% cloud managed. The Eagle Eye Cloud Security Camera Video Management System supports a broad array of IP and analog cameras uses Intelligent Bandwidth Management™, making it easy to deploy at single and multiple sites. The API platform uses the Eagle Eye, Big Data Video Framework™, with time based data structures used for indexing, search, retrieval and analysis of the live and archived video.
