Amazon Web Services (AWS) has achieved the DoD Provisional Authorization under the US Department of Defense Cloud Security Model, at security impact levels 3 through 5—making the cloud provider much more accessible to government agencies who work with classified information.

AWS had previously received a DoD Provisional Authorization for security impact levels 1 through 2, but this new Authorization covers means that DoD customers can now move forward with their deployments of official use only unclassified information.

DoD employees and federal contractors can now request DoD compliance support by submitting a FedRAMP/DoD Compliance Support Request and begin to moving through the authorization process to achieve a DoD ATO all of the Levels 1-5 with AWS.
