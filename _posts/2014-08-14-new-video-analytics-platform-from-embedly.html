Embeddable tool provider Embed.ly has launched a new video analytics platform called Bale.io. The new platform let you see how people are actually watching a video, whether it is from YouTube, Vimeo, Vine, Instagram and more.

You do not have to own the video, you just add the URL into Bale.io, and you get a new link or embed script which you can use to embed video on your website.

Once Bale is use, you will be able to see how people are watching, sharing and interacting with videos you have posted on your website, no matter which platform the video originates from, regardless of whether you own the video or not.
