Atlanta-based financial technology company Payscape announced today that it has added integrated donation capabilities to its proprietary eCommerce platform, Vendevor. The donation features provide a web-based outlet for both businesses and nonprofits, streamlining the process of receiving charitable contributions. The product update aims to target organizations such as nonprofits, schools, churches, kickstarters and crowdfunding endeavors.

Thousands of companies already use Payscape’s line of SaaS products to compliment their business needs. The addition of donation capabilities is an opportunity to expand the existing financial technology options that Payscape offers.

Some of the most anticipated features within the new donation capabilities include variant contribution, recurring contribution, email integration, and the highly-visual donation progress bar. These features are specifically designed to meet the needs of every philanthropic effort.

In addition to website-based giving, Payscape can also integrate donation capabilities directly to an organization’s Facebook page. With a simple add-on, companies can now accept and monitor donation amounts along with the color-coded progress bar.
“This feature allows Payscape to provide services to a full spectrum of industries. We’re passionate about supporting nonprofits, and with the technology to accept recurring donations through a variety of templates and payment options, we hope to achieve increased donations for all of our nonprofit partners,” said Doug Hardman, Chief Technology Officer.

Learn more at vendevor.com/donations.
