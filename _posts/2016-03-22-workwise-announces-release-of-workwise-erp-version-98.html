WorkWise, a leading ERP and CRM software developer, announced today a new release of its WorkWise ERP product offering. WorkWise ERP Version 9.8 continues the tradition of the innovative Generation 9 product line, adding over 50 enhancements for improved performance, enhanced mobility and greater ease-of-use.

To provide manufacturers with intuitive access into shop floor scheduling features, WorkWise ERP Version 9.8 includes a new Visual Schedulers Workbench that incorporates Gantt Chart style scheduling forms, drag and drop functionality and visual indicators for material availability.

A new Production Dispatch & Reporting mobile app delivers an online dispatch and data collection system accessible from any mobile device, tablet or web browser. This new application incorporates a variety of enhancements over the previous shop data collection module and provides supervisors with a birds-eye view into employee and shop floor performance.

“The release of WorkWise ERP Version 9.8 encompasses a unique combination of value-added functionality enhancements as well as technology advancements to help our customers achieve greater operational productivity and growth,” states Wayne Wedell, WorkWise President and CEO. “It also lays the foundation for future advancements to functionality and user interface, for upcoming releases.”

Additional significant features included in the release of WorkWise ERP Version 9.8 include:

  • Enhanced Routing Maintenance functionality added to the Engineering Workbench provides check in/out capabilities with visual change indicators.
  • Numerous enhancements to streamline the process from Quote to Cash for Job Shops.
  • New Progress Billing and Deposit functions to manage long projects with milestone billings.
  • A new Inventory Analysis feature in Business Metrics that allows users to create both current and historical views of inventory activity and valuation.
  • A suite of eCommerce APIs that allow clients to integrate with third party developers for web-sites that retrieve and send data with WorkWise ERP.

About WorkWise
WorkWise is a leading developer of Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) and Customer Relationship Management (CRM) software solutions along with comprehensive implementation and support services. Our flagship products, WorkWise ERP and Oncontact CRM are easy-to-use, quick to implement and provide a high return on investment. Use of WorkWise software applications provides more accurate and timely information that helps enable companies to grow.

WorkWise ERP offers make-to-order, repetitive and mixed-mode solutions to manufacturers to shorten cycle times and optimize manufacturing information, supply chain management, and manufacturing execution and planning. WorkWise ERP enhances operations by taking time out of the business processes and increasing value-add in products and services.

OnContact CRM, which is offered with either a hosted or on premise deployment, is an award-winning, browser-based CRM solution. As a full-featured, customizable application suite that automates the sales, marketing and customer service areas of companies, OnContact CRM has been awarded the TopTenREVIEWS Excellence Award in recognition of its unique design and superior features.

In addition, WorkWise has pre-configured integration between WorkWise ERP and OnContact CRM, which is unavailable from other ERP and CRM suppliers.

WorkWise is headquartered near Milwaukee, WI and has been inspiring companies to achieve new levels of success for over 15 years.
