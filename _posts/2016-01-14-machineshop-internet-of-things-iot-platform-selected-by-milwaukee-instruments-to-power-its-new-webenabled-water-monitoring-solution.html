MachineShop, the industry’s only API-based Internet of Things (IoT) platform provider, today announced its partnership with Milwaukee Instruments to power its new web-enabled, digital controllers for water application management. Milwaukee Instruments is a global provider of electrochemical instrumentation for water analysis and provides precision, diagnostic equipment used in laboratories, food and beverage operations, agriculture, industry, and by hobbyists in a wide variety of applications.

Milwaukee Instruments’ new offering leapfrogs alternative products in the market as it includes remote connectivity, an intuitive web portal, data filtering, business rules, alerting, reporting, integrated billing and more. Unlike other platforms, MachineShop’s approach gave the customer total control and ownership of the application and all the underlying components and services.

“We embraced the customer imperative for real-time data and remote monitoring,” said Bryan Moore, General Manager at Milwaukee Instruments. “MachineShop’s middleware service not only allowed us total ownership of our application, but provided tremendous flexibility as we add advanced features in the future such as a multi-location/multi-unit enterprise management portal.”

According to MachineShop CEO Michael Campbell, “The innovation by Milwaukee Instruments exemplifies the endless ways that IoT technology is transforming the Industrial Internet and can provide competitive advantage to companies of any size.”

About Milwaukee Instruments
Milwaukee Instruments is comprised of manufacturing, sales, and distribution facilities throughout the world. Their precision, diagnostic equipment is used in laboratories, food and beverage operations, agriculture, industry, and by hobbyists in a wide variety of applications. The company manufactures affordable, easy-to-use meters, testers, monitors and controllers for measuring pH, EC, TDS, ORP, LUX, DO and all types of photometers and mini-titrators. Milwaukee Instruments offers equipment built to the highest international standards; fast, knowledgeable customer service; and an excellent warranty program – all at competitive prices. For more information, visit http://www.milwaukeeinstruments.com.
