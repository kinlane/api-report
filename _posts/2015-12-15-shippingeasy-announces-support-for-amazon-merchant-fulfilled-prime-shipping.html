ShippingEasy’s integration with Amazon’s Buy Shipping Service allows Amazon sellers to leverage ShippingEasy’s fulfillment and shipping tools across all orders, including: 

  • Same day fulfillment of Amazon Prime orders
  • Free Endicia account for USPS labels
  • Easy integration of USPS, FedEx, UPS and DHL --all carriers in single system
  • Access to USPS CPP pricing on all qualifying orders
  • AutoShip artificial shipping intelligence
  • Batch or 1 by 1 shipping labels
  • Customized packing slips and labels
  • Extensive shipping rules to eliminate duplicative actions
  • Scan based return labels
  • Tailored reporting across all sales channels

“ShippingEasy was built to consolidate all of an eCommerce merchant’s selling channels and carriers into a single system with powerful shipping tools. Amazon sellers require a robust solution that appreciates and complies with that platform’s set guidelines. The new Amazon Buy Shipping API integration ensures compliant Amazon Prime order fulfillment within ShippingEasy. Sellers can fulfill orders across all channels and all carriers in a single system while fully complying with the Amazon Prime promise, including same day shipping,” says Katie May, CEO of ShippingEasy.

ShippingEasy long-time Amazon customers are delighted with the integration due to the convenience of shipping all orders from a single system, plus time savings of 50%.

“Amazon is one of our largest sales channels and thanks to ShippingEasy helping us fulfill and ship our orders on time and accurately we have the pleasure of being part of the Amazon Merchant Fulfilled Prime (MFP) program. However, the processing requirements are different than before and shipping was required to be purchased through Amazon. We reached out to ShippingEasy and they developed MFP support so all shipping can be done in one place versus having to process some shipments in Amazon and the rest in ShippingEasy. MFP fulfillment through Amazon took us about 6 hours on average. Now that ShippingEasy has integrated with the MFP program we fulfill these prime orders in 3 hours or less,” said Jacob Baum of Fastener Source Inc.
