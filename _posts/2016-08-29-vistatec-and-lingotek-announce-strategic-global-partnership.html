Vistatec, a world leader in global content solutions, and Lingotek | The Translation Network a premier cloud based translation network solution provider today announce a global strategic partnership which will see the two companies closely collaborate in the delivery of best of breed translation workflow technology and content repository integration together with widely acclaimed premiere quality, innovative language services.

Phil Ritchie, Vistatec’s Chief Technology Officer explains: “One of the initial questions we get from prospective customers relates to the ability to integrate with content repositories of all kinds. Lingotek’s platform has an unparalleled number of these ‘out-of-the-box’ solutions supported by comprehensive Multilingual APIs which will complement those that we have built ourselves here at Vistatec.”

Lingotek’s platform and Vistatec’s open source editor already use common source code libraries and conform to industry standards such as XLIFF. Ritchie continued: “We share a collective vision with Lingotek of how globalization platforms and applications should be designed and built and the open, interoperable standards and production scenarios that they support.”

"There’s a lot of synergy between Lingotek and Vistatec," said Rob Vandenberg, President and CEO of Lingotek. "We both offer innovative, tech-forward solutions in the translation services industry. We also share some of the same clients, so it’s a natural fit. We are pleased to partner with a translation services provider that is so well respected in the industry. "

“Customers who are looking for the independence and agility of privately-held industry leaders with a track record of providing some of the world’s most iconic brands with premiere solutions will, I believe, be very interested in the potential of this synergistic relationship which combines a common ethos of agility, energy, quality and positive commitment.” concluded Vistatec CEO, Thomas Murray.

Vistatec is excited about this partnership with Lingotek, and the extended flexibility it will bring to our customer solutions and programs.

About Vistatec
Vistatec has been supporting some of the world's most iconic brands to optimize their global commercial potential since 1997. Vistatec is one of the world's leading localization solutions providers headquartered in Dublin, Ireland with offices in Mountain View, California, USA.

About Lingotek
Lingotek's Translation Network is the only cloud-based solution to connect all your global content in one place, giving you the power to manage your brand worldwide. Our industry-leading technology pairs with the best enterprise applications to continuously push dynamic multilingual content to all of your global markets. Lingotek is based in Lehi, Utah-- also known as Silicon Slopes--and is funded by Signal Peak Ventures and In-Q-Tel.
