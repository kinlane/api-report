United World Telecom (UWT), a leading provider of international toll free and worldwide local numbers, is celebrating its 20th year in business and posted its largest revenue numbers in 2015.

UWT continues to see year over year revenue gains with a strong track record of providing many new products and services and customized corporate solutions while increasing its core business model. In all, United World Telecom has continued to release and enhance a wide variety of telecom solutions for businesses and individuals for over two decades.

Two decades of experience have transformed United World Telecom from a new venture in callback solutions with big ideas into a successful provider of local and toll free numbers to small businesses, multinational corporations, and individuals worldwide. Thousands of businesses in more than 150 countries, rely on our telecommunication solutions to improve their operations in local markets around the world.

United World Telecom provides its core function to leading companies by offering the following comprehensive features and advantages:

  • The largest selections of local and toll free numbers worldwide
  • A full suite of customizable Call Forwarding services to meet business-specific needs, regardless of company size and location.
  • A state-of-the-art telecom infrastructure with reliable local and international partners for highest service quality.
  • 24/7 professional customer support with priority escalation procedures to ensure quick resolution of issues.
  • Continuously evolving advanced API for handling complex requirements of multinational corporations.
