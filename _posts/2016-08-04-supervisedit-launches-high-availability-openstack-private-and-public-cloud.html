SupervisedIT, a provider of managed cloud hosting, has announced the introduction of a new zero-downtime public and private cloud platform based on OpenStack. SupervisedIT is part of the ServerMania group, and benefits from ServerMania’s decade-long experience of building high-performance, infrastructure hosting platforms for business.

SupervisedIT’s OpenStack public cloud platform provides high-availability virtual compute and storage infrastructure with instant server creation and scaling. Infrastructure control is fast and intuitive with SupervisedIT’s bespoke cloud web interface, and infrastructure-as-code control is provided by OpenStack’s flexible and comprehensive API.

Engineered for redundancy, performance, and scalability, SupervisedIT’s private cloud platform combines the enhanced agility and efficiency of a virtual platform with the security and control of wholly private physical infrastructure. SupervisedIT’s custom OpenStack private clouds are more cost effective than on-premise physical infrastructure.

Housed in a SSAE 16 Type II-certified data center, SupervisedIT’s 10 Gbit per second infrastructure is capable of providing data transfer rates up to 10-times faster than traditional cloud service providers. All data is stored on high-availability SAN Storage Servers equipped with fast SSD drives to ensure low-latency access and constant availability.

“Our clients have diverse infrastructure hosting requirements, and we have built a cloud platform engineered to provide the flexibility and choice that enterprise organizations demand,” explained CEO, Kevin Blanchard, “Public, private, or hybrid cloud — each user can deploy the infrastructure they need with full confidence that our redundant high-availability platform will provide the constant uptime and exceptional performance they rely on.”

With SupervisedIT’s OpenStack platform, businesses can choose the cloud modality that serves their needs, backed by the company’s long experience of building infrastructure platforms with unbeatable uptime and availability.

OpenStack is the leading open source infrastructure management solution with powerful compute, storage, and and network management capabilities. Its thriving developer ecosystem and API, which is fast becoming a de facto industry standard, gives businesses the tools they need to innovate while optimizing their infrastructure investment.
