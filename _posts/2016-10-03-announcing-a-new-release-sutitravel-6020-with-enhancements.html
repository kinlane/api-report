SutiSoft, a leading provider of business management solutions, today announces the release of SutiTravel 6.0.20. The latest version includes additional features designed to meet the requirements of travel agents and travelers.

Key Enhancements
Configuring Credit Card for Payment
SutiTravel allows travel agents to configure a credit card number through which payment can be done for bookings. Currently, the feature is made available for the Abacus service and will be implemented for other service providers as well.Â Â Â Â 

Accessing Hotel Inventory from Multiple APIs
SutiTravel allows travel agents to access hotel inventory from multiple service providers. Partial Cancellation
SutiTravel now supports partial cancellations, which enables a user to cancel a single ticket instead of cancelling the entire booking. This is convenient when booking tickets in a group. Currently, the feature is available for Galileo/Hermes corporate services and can be implemented for other services as well.

Adding Credit to the Wallet
Offer credit balances for flights and hotel bookings. Adding credit to the wallet helps users book flights and hotels when sufficient funds are not available.

Car Integration
SutiTravel is integrated with car services to allow travelers to book cars directly from the website.

For more information, please visit https://www.sutitravel.com
