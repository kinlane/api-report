HelloSign today announced it has launched HelloSign for Salesforce on the Salesforce AppExchange, empowering businesses to connect with their customers, partners and employees in entirely new ways. HelloSign for Salesforce makes it possible for teams of all sizes to seamlessly upload, populate, send and track important businesses documents requiring legally binding signatures directly from Salesforce.

Built on the Salesforce App Cloud, HelloSign for Salesforce is currently available on the AppExchange at https://appexchange.salesforce.com/listingDetail?listingId=a0N3A00000EFmGIUA1

HelloSign for Salesforce Key Features
-Send contracts for signature: Users can send sales contracts for legally binding signature directly from Salesforce.
-Easily track the status of signatures: The document status is automatically tracked in the Notes & Attachments section making follow up simple. Users can see exactly when their documents have been viewed or signed.
-Auto-populate high-volume documents: Pre-fill any documents with Salesforce data using HelloSign templates and merge field functionality.
-Create templates without leaving Salesforce: Users can set up frequently used documents once in Salesforce and reuse as necessary.
-Convenient access: Users can access eSignature functionality from anywhere within Salesforce including Contacts, Leads, Accounts and Opportunities, making it easy to issue an agreement from anywhere in the application.
-Business Branding: Easily add a company logo and name to email notifications and signature requests for a personal and professional feel.
