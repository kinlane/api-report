NYMBUS, the world’s first complete, full-stack, API driven core banking platform, has been selected as one of 400 fintech developers showcasing their innovation at FinDEVr New York 2016. The company is presenting its technology on March 30 at 2:45 p.m.

NYMBUS provides the most advanced core processing platform in a secure, cloud-based, full-stack product offering that includes all of the mission-critical functions financial institutions need. NYMBUS’ revolutionary platform enables financial institutions to effectively respond to today’s digital revolution and shifting consumer demand for omnichannel banking.

Along with showcasing its core banking platform, NYMBUS will also announce its concept of the ‘NYMBUS App Store’ at FinDEVr, where developers will be able to create applications for bankers and their customers using NYMBUS APIs and style guides. By addressing new ways consumers want to bank, NYMBUS is better positioning today’s small and mid-sized financial institutions with the tools they need to stand out and thrive in the marketplace.

“We are honored to have been selected as one of the top fintech innovators to present on the FinDEVr stage,” said NYMBUS’ CEO Alex Lopatine. “We are excited to have brought our revolutionary idea to life, and the interest we’re receiving from small and mid-sized banks and credit unions across the nation is exactly what we anticipated.”

NYMBUS was chosen to present in part based on their specific technology and how it solves a global problem of outdated technology affecting banks and credit unions. Other factors included the company’s development stage, number of employees, financial data, key partnerships, customer traction and its well-known executive leadership team, who are all fintech industry disruptors.

“We are very proud that NYMBUS has been selected to present for our ingenuity at FinDEVr,” said NYMBUS EVP & Chief Strategy Officer David Mitchell. “Learning about new products, new ways of accomplishing work and new delivery mechanisms is what makes the conference crucial to new technology leaders. FinDEVr allows the disruptive developers and architects, who have their own brand of ingenuity, to turn their concepts into reality.”

FinDEVr New York is a key arena for fintech developers to share their latest tools, platforms and case studies with other developers and heavy-hitters in the financial services community. Presentations focus on the future of financial, banking and payments technology.

“With all the talk of fintech strategy and disruption, we’re pleased to bring leading, innovative minds like NYMBUS together under one roof,” said Finovate CEO Eric Mattson.

To learn more about FinDEVr, visit newyork2016.findevr.com. For more information on how NYMBUS, together with its partners, is revolutionizing the banking industry, visit nymbus.com.
