Scriptr.io, a powerful cloud-based on-premise IoT application development platform company, is demonstrating an integration of Stream Technologies IoT-X platform and MultiTech’s MultiConnect® Conduit™ LoRaWAN gateway at the Momenta Partners pavilion (booth 731) and Stream Technologies’ booth 217 at IoT World in Santa Clara, CA, May 11-12.

“The MultiConnect Conduit gateway opens new opportunities for connecting IoT devices to the network since LoRaWAN-based devices promise greater range, longer battery life and lower cost,” said Daniel Quant, ‎Vice President, Product Management and Strategic Marketing at MultiTech. “As a part of our commitment to simplify the IoT, we are pleased to work with application development platform providers like Scriptr.io to streamline the development and integration of LoRa-based applications.”

Alan Tait, Stream's CTO commented, "We are excited to work with MultiTech and Scriptr.io to showcase IoT applications. We’ve supported MultiTech’s Conduit LoRaWAN Gateway on our IoT-X connectivity platform, and by also combining and utilising Scriptr.io it creates a powerful full stack solution for IoT.”

Rabih Nassar, Founder and CEO of Scriptr.io said, "We’re enthused to work with MultiTech and Stream Technologies to offer our IoT development platform to what I expect to be a very healthy market of MultiTech’s MultiConnect Conduit LoRaWAN gateway developers and integrators."

About Stream Technologies
Stream Technologies, headquartered in the UK, is the company behind IoT-X. IoT-X is an agile/API-based connectivity enablement, monitoring, billing and rules based decision making platform. Designed for all operators of cellular, satellite & Low Power Wide Area Networks (including LoRaWAN), IoT-X is offered as a Platform as a Service (PaaS) and is fast becoming recognised as the most innovative PaaS in IoT. IoT-X offers low-cost, low-risk, high-functionality of connectivity management, resulting in fast time-to-market, with support from one of the most experienced technical and operational teams in IoT. http://www.stream-technologies.com/

About Scriptr.io
Scriptr.io is a powerful cloud-based or on-premise IoT application development platform that allows developers and integrators build elegant IoT applications that benefit from cloud scalability and JavaScript simplicity. The scriptr.io JavaScript based development environment has pre-built connectors to major IoT platforms and enterprise systems, enabling customers to accelerate development and time-to-market, providing an important competitive advantage. Scriptr.io is a carrier grade application engine delivered to several mobile operators, supporting millions of users. https://www.scriptr.io/
