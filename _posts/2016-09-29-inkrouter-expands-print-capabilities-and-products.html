InkRouter, a print procurement software solution for web-to-print storefronts, is introducing even more materials and products to its print offerings. Newly formed partnerships from SGIA Expo 16, a trade show for the imaging industry held Sept. 14-16, effectively expand the custom printing capabilities of InkRouter’s global network of print providers.

InkRouter will be working with 14 regional manufacturing facilities throughout the continental U.S. to provide consumers with a wider array of custom print products. Added options will include fully customizable coffee mugs printed on a high-volume, direct-to-garment sublimation oven as well as custom T-shirts in several qualities and sizes.

The print procurement automation software uses bi-directional API technology to facilitate print orders between print providers and print resellers. Its algorithmic adaptive intelligence matches orders to print providers within the network based on JDF/CIP4 compatible XML definitions. The API service is currently provided at no charge to the industry.

InkRouter recently presented its software for print at Graph Expo 16, the premier event for the graphic communications industry held Sept. 25-28 in Orlando, Fla.

About InkRouterSM
InkRouter, Inc. is a wholly owned subsidiary of Opensoft Inc., located in Las Vegas, Nev. Opensoft Inc. has advanced technologies for the print industry and extensive experience in developing print software for managing print processes at every stage of selling, manufacturing and fulfillment.
