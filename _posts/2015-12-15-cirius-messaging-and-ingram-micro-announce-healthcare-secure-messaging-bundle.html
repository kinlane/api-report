Cirius Messaging Inc., a provider of cloud-based private email encryption, secure file sharing and advanced message control, in partnership with Ingram Micro, the world’s largest distributor of technology products, today announced a new secure messaging bundle for healthcare which comprises Cirius’ secure messaging solution and Microsoft Office 365.

Created specifically for healthcare organizations such as medical and dental practices, hospitals, pharmaceutical companies and medical device manufacturers the secure healthcare bundle gives channel partners a simple, effective way to maximize profits and garner new opportunities. By offering their healthcare customers a secure, intuitive cloud-based service that enhances and integrates seamlessly with the Microsoft Office 365 platform, channel partners add tremendous value while ensuring crucial regulatory compliance, data privacy and advanced message control. The easy-to-use Cirius solution fully secures private emails, encrypts large files and controls messages sent to internal employees, external providers and patients on any device.

”Bundling Microsoft Office 365 and Cirius Secure Messaging encryption into a single solution makes it easier for WCC IT, as a reseller, to quickly create an effective solution for our healthcare customers,” said Brent Sadler, CEO of WCC IT. “Most importantly, it gives our customers the ability to communicate internally, with other healthcare organizations and even with patients while complying with regulatory requirements.”

Cirius’ secure messaging solution is differentiated by its:

  • Unique, true-cloud architecture that requires no configuration of existing email infrastructure, no MX records to transfer and no gateway to install and manage.
  • Advanced message control, which includes tracking, delivery slip and other advanced control features.
  • Ability to secure messages on any device, including smartphones, tablets and laptops.
  • Seamless integration with Microsoft Outlook, Office 365 OWA and other browser-based webmail clients.
  • Simplified guest user registration and user experience with a web-based interface that precludes the need for any downloads or complex gateway installations.
  • Powerful APIs for integration into secure webforms, fax replacement, email archive repositories and other cloud platforms.
  • Simple “one price” subscription model that requires no substantial upfront expense or complicated add-on features.

“We are excited about the opportunity this bundle to enhance Office 365 and present channel partners an option to provide healthcare organizations with secure email and file sharing on virtually any device,” said Josef Lara, SVP Business Development, Cirius Messaging.

Now available in the channel, the Microsoft 365 secure healthcare bundle with Cirius encryption can be found at Ingram Micro by visiting us.cloud.im.
