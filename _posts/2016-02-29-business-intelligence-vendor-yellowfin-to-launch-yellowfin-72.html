Yellowfin 7.2 will be officially launched in a series of Webinars, scheduled throughout Wednesday 16 March 2016. Register for the official Webinar launch HERE: http://www.yellowfinbi.com/YFCommunityNews-Yellowfin-7-2-launch-Webinar-Make-your-data-valuable-for-everyone-217889

“Yellowfin 7.2 will deliver the broadest, most shareable BI platform on the planet,” said Yellowfin Director of Product Marketing, John Ryan. “Yellowfin 7.2 has been engineered to support a range of BI users and use cases, enabling organizations to establish and benefit from a company-wide culture of data-driven decision-making.”

According to a wealth of industry research, BI adoption rates have remained relatively stagnant, at around 25 percent, for the last decade. Ryan said that Yellowfin 7.2 would deliver “significant new functionality” to address this lingering industry trend.

“Yellowfin 7.2 boosts user adoption rates, and therefore the value of BI deployments, by empowering people to work together collaboratively throughout the data analysis and content creation process,” said Ryan. “Yellowfin 7.2 enables everyone to benefit from BI insights – no matter their job title or technical skills.”

Ryan said Yellowfin 7.2 would encompass a range of major additions and improvements, including:

  • Business Workflow: Enhanced collaboration to improve task management and analytic workflow, connecting BI consumers with business analysts, to produce better, faster data-driven insights for all user types
  • Connectors: API connectors for a range of third-party Web-based applications and data sources, including Salesforce, Google Analytics, Twitter and more
  • Forecasting: Improved forecasting and trend analysis

Yellowfin will host three concise 40-minute Yellowfin 7.2 release Webinars throughout Wednesday 16 March 2016.

About Yellowfin
Yellowfin is a global Business Intelligence (BI) and analytics software vendor passionate about making BI easy. Founded in 2003 in response to the complexity and costs associated with implementing and using traditional BI tools, Yellowfin is a highly intuitive 100 percent Web-based reporting and analytics solution. Yellowfin is a leader in mobile, collaborative and embedded BI, as well as Location Intelligence and data visualization.

Over 10,000 organizations, and more than 1.5 million end-users across 70 different countries, use Yellowfin every day. For more information, visit http://www.yellowfinbi.com
