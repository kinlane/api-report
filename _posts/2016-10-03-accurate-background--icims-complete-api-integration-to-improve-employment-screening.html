Accurate Background, the fastest growing and most trusted provider of employment background screening, today announced the introduction of an API integration with iCIMS, Inc., a leading provider of Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) talent acquisition solutions.

Accurate Background, specializing in employment background screening and drug testing services, now offers an improved integration with the iCIMS Talent Platform, a suite of talent acquisition tools that enables organizations to attract, screen, and hire the best talent for their ever-changing business needs. The upgraded integration enriches the customer experience by offering a seamless transfer of candidate background screening data from the Accurate Background platform into the iCIMS Talent Platform, where recruiters can manage the hiring process from start to finish, all within a single, cloud-based application.

“Our API integration allows the instant availability of Accurate Background’s solutions within the iCIMS Talent Platform,” said Scott Hebert, chief revenue officer at Accurate Background. “This addition strengthens our already excellent partnership with iCIMS and furthers our dedication to providing reliable background screening services to our family of mutual clients and prospects.”    

“As over half of the information presented on a resume may be false or misleading, companies must thoroughly investigate a candidate’s background before extending an employment offer to help minimize potential risk,” said Michael Wilczak, senior vice president, strategy and corporate development of iCIMS. “Through our enhanced integration with Accurate Background, we look to improve our customers’ background screening experience to enable them to make more informed hiring decisions.”

Accurate Background will benefit from UNIFi, iCIMS’ Platform-as-a-Service framework that enables partners to integrate their software applications with the iCIMS Talent Acquisition Suite and market their products to iCIMS’ customers through the recently launched iCIMS Marketplace. The iCIMS Marketplace, iCIMS’ online environment featuring the largest partner ecosystem in talent acquisition, enables companies to shop for and engage with supporting technology providers that are the best fit for their talent acquisition and management needs – all in one logical, easy-to-use interface. Additionally, through UNIFi, partners can build long-term, lasting relationships through standard APIs that offer flexible data sharing, adding value to their customer experience.
