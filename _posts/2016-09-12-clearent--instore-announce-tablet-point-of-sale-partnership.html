Clearent, one of the fastest-growing payment processing companies in the U.S., and Instore, one of the world’s easiest-to-use POS systems, today announced a partnership. The companies have integrated their respective solutions so that merchants everywhere can enjoy Instore’s intelligent design and full suite of POS features alongside Clearent’s end-to-end payments platform.

Instore is a complete, cloud-based point-of-sale system that works on an iPad. With Instore, merchants can make sales, manage staff, market their business, sell gift cards and reward customers – all through one system and one device. Instore offers two solutions, Terminal and Pro, and is a great fit for a wide variety of restaurant and retail businesses.

With 10 years of experience in payments, Clearent was founded on honesty and transparency and provides clear pricing with no hidden fees. In addition, merchants can get their money faster with Clearent’s next day funding service, which features an 11 p.m. Eastern cut-off time, which is one of the latest in the industry. Clearent is a robust one-stop shop for business owners looking to accept payments in-store, online, and on the go, and offers full merchant services. With Clearent’s robust reseller network and direct access to merchants, the partnership will help drive Instore into new and existing verticals.

When asked about Instore’s new partnership with Clearent, Instore CEO Matt Niehaus said, “We’re excited to see Clearent’s product suite help us welcome more merchants to the Instore family. Clearent’s API was simple and easy to use, and their concise documentation made the integration very easy.”

“After evaluating a number of mPOS software platforms, we were very impressed with Instore’s intuitive design. They understand merchant needs and workflows and have built a powerful alternative to the common POS solutions that you see in the marketplace,” commented Dan Geraty, CEO of Clearent.

About Instore
Instore is a single, simple operating system for retail businesses. Starting with point of sale, the Instore Pro platform includes labor management, customer rewards, CRM, marketing, gift cards, reporting, bar code scanning and more. Merchants seeking an even simpler solution can use Instore Terminal, which combines payments, gift cards, rewards and CRM for one low price. Merchants in a wide variety of industries save time and money by managing operations on Instore while growing sales through integrated features that inspire repeat customer visits.
